About Us

We the LionBots, Team #7245, are an FRC (FIRST Robotics Competition) team

Achievements:

  • Participating in the 2022 San Francisco FRC, creating a robot that solved the engineering design problem and earning the Judges Award.
  • Volunteering in the 2022 Cal-Games, setting up the field for competitors to showcase their robots and make sure all competitors were on time and active at the competition.

We the LionBots #7245 are an FRC(FIRST Robotics Competition) team representing Kipp King Collegiate High School located at 2005 Via Barrett, San Lorenzo, CA 94580
